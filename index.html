Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has been making strides in innovation across various sectors, with advancements driven by technology, entrepreneurship, and social development.

Sustainability

Bangladesh, as one of the most climate-vulnerable countries in the world, has embraced sustainability as a key focus in its development efforts.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure of Bangladesh has been developing steadily to support its growing population, urbanization, and economic growth.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made remarkable progress in empowering its people through initiatives that focus on social, economic, political, and gender equality.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh is on a transformative journey, envisioning a future that emphasizes sustainable development, inclusive growth, technological advancement, and resilience against global challenges. The country's future vision is driven by long-term strategic plans and aspirations to elevate itself into a prosperous, equitable, and self-reliant nation.

Development and Vision

Bangladesh can achieve its Vision 2041 to become a high-income, developed nation, but this will require overcoming several challenges. The country has shown impressive growth in recent years, supported by strong exports, remittances, and infrastructure development.

Climate and Sustainability

Bangladesh is already demonstrating leadership in climate resilience, with initiatives like the Bangladesh Delta Plan 2100, the creation of the Climate Change Trust Fund, and its investments in disaster management and renewable energy. These efforts position the country as a model for other nations.

Governance and Corruption

Establishing transparent and accountable governance is critical for Bangladesh’s equitable development. While progress has been made in areas like digitizing public services (e.g., Digital Bangladesh Initiative) and improving infrastructure, systemic challenges remain.

Young people should think about what kind of world they want to live in and start working to make it happen. Government frequently encourages youth to become changemakers by engaging in social entrepreneurship and tackling global challenges. We focus on reshaping the world through compassion, innovation, and entrepreneurship, inspiring a more inclusive and sustainable future for all.
